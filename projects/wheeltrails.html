Wheeltrails (R&D)

A geo-located, digital, floor-based street art project, inspired by the water trails left by wheelchairs on the ground and using motion unique to the movement of mobility wheels to draw intentional marks in the digital space, viewable by others through phones or projection. Wheel trails puts street art into indoor spaces and venues, interjecting questions on disability, representation, access, and perceptions of activity.
