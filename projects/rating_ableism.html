LLama Ableism - 2024

Materials: Tabletop roleplaying game & LLMs.

Themes:AI, Disability.

Examining a LLMs mapping of Ableism to various words. Forcing the LLM to provide a score for how Ableist it rates various words. This is performed for words it selects and for words that it makes up. Using made-up words can provide deeper insight as it bypasses filters re-enforced in building the LLM.
