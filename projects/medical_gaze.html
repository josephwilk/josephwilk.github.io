Medical Gaze - 2022

Materials: Photographs / Programming code

Exporation of the Wellcome Trust archive as a found material through programming code & face detection to deconstruct and re-create work that connected with my own experiences of disability. I explored search terms around wheelchair and mobility aids focused on the image archive. While my intention was not to find doctors the results where dominated by portraits of doctors. It became apparent through analysing the images using facial recognition the difference in the portrayal of the suffering of the patient and the prestige of doctors within the collection. This connected strongly with my own experiences of the dehumanising medical gaze, being treated as a medical problem to be fixed without regard to the individual or their desires.
