Creatures Collaborative Songbook

Part of the Bath Fringe Festival 2019, run by Mob-Handed Press.

A series of collaborative songbooks created by selected musicians around a central theme. Artists contribute one song each, which are then anthologised into a zine. This publication is launched with a night of performance and storytelling.

It is a communication between artist and audience, and an exploration of how people visualise the music that they write, perform, and consume.

The theme was Creatures:

Creatures of habit,
Uncontrollable beasts,
The ones within…
And the ones without.

My submission which combines a songsheet in code form and the live visuals it creates.
